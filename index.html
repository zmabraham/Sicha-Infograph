The Three Events of Shavuos

Matan Torah, the histalkus of Dovid HaMelech, and the histalkus of the Baal Shem Tov — three Shavuot events united by the framework of "העליונים ירדו לתחתונים / התחתונים יעלו לעליונים."

The Angels' Claim to the Torah

When Moshe ascended Sinai, the angels claimed the Torah as their own under dina d'bar metzra. The Rebbe examines five answers from the meforshim — and finds the true answer in Moshe's own words: yishuv adif, dirah b'tachtonim.
